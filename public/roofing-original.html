Welcome to Peak Roofing

Peak Roofing has been proudly serving the Springfield, IL area since 1998. As a family-owned and operated business, we take pride in treating every customer like a neighbor. Whether you need a complete roof replacement or a simple repair, our team of certified professionals is here to help.

We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured for your protection. We use only the highest quality materials from trusted manufacturers like GAF, Owens Corning, and CertainTeed. All of our work is backed by comprehensive manufacturer warranties AND our own workmanship guarantee.
